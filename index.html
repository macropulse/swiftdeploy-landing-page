  • AI agents write code but generate zero infrastructure

Cloud-Native Architecture, Agent-First Experience

SwiftDeploy combines developer-friendly simplicity with production-grade cloud deployment—giving you full control over your infrastructure.

Agent-First Interface

Conversational MCP tools that understand natural language. Just say "Deploy my app to staging"—SwiftDeploy handles the rest, asking clarifying questions when needed.

AI Architecture Engine

Knowledge graph of 500+ deployment patterns generates optimal cloud architectures per codebase type. Auto-configured for best practices and cost optimization.

Infrastructure as Code

Production-grade Terraform files stored in your own infrastructure. You own your code—no vendor lock-in, complete transparency, full control.

Zero-Knowledge Security

Secrets stored in cloud-native secret managers, never exposed to AI agents. Cross-account access with least-privilege permissions. Your credentials, your control.

Cost Optimized

Defaults to ARM-based instances for better price-performance. Intelligent resource sizing and auto-scaling—optimizing your cloud spend without sacrificing performance.

Continuous Coaching

Proactive DevOps guidance during development. Detects missing env vars, Dockerfile issues, pending migrations—before they break production.
